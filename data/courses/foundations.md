# Foundations

Build the professional foundation you need to lead with technology.

## Exemplar
A professional portfolio published on WordPress that weaves together
a personal identity statement, a technology stack analysis, a focus
and self-management plan, a social impact proposal, and an influence
map — each section demonstrating the learner's authentic perspective,
not a template. The portfolio reads as one coherent narrative: who I am,
what I use, how I work, what I care about, and who I need to know.

## Learning Objectives
- Can identify interests, values, and strengths that shape professional identity
- Can explain how audience and context influence professional communication
- Can draft and revise a statement of professional motivation
- Can launch a WordPress Playground instance and publish content
- Can explain the four freedoms of open source software
- Can identify target role tools and distinguish known vs. needed technology
- Can organize a personal technology stack
- Can recognize focus patterns and apply task initiation strategies
- Can identify a community to support and articulate an impact goal
- Can identify field organizations and key individuals for professional influence
